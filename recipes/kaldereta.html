KALDERETA

Description

Kaldereta or caldereta is a goat meat stew from the Philippines. Variations of the dish use beef, chicken, or pork. Commonly, the goat meat is stewed with vegetables and liver paste. Vegetables may include tomatoes, potatoes, olives, bell peppers, and hot peppers. Kaldereta sometimes includes tomato sauce. Learn more

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs meat(pork/beef) cubed
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves minced or crushed
  • 1 piece onion finely chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 piece green bell pepper sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup liver spread processed using blender
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 cups potatoes sliced
  • 2 cups carrots sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2/3 cups green olives
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in the cooking pot or pressure cooker
  2. Saute the onion and garlic
  3. Add the meat. Cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown
  4. Add dry bay leaves and crushed pepper. Stir
  5. Add the liver spread, stir
  6. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth
  7. Cook the meat until in becomes tender(about 30 minutes if using pressure cooker, or 1 to 2 hours if using ordinary pot)
  8. Add potato and carrots, cook for 8 to 10 minutes
  9. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot. Stir and continue to cook for 5 minutes more
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste
  11. Serve Hot. Enjoy!
